HuBar is a visual analytics tool for summarizing and comparing task performance sessions in augmented reality.

It highlights correlations between cognitive workload and performer motion data, making it easier to inspect sessions over time.

![HuBar teaser]({{site.url}}/images/vis_portfolio/hubar_teaser.png)

**Focus**: AR guidance, multimodal behavior analysis, and workload-aware visual analytics.
