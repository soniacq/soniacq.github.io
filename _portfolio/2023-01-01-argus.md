ARGUS enables the interactive exploration and debugging of the data ecosystem needed to support intelligent task guidance.

It supports both online and offline workflows so users can inspect task performance while the task is happening or after the fact.

**Focus**: explainability, debugging, and multimodal AI-assisted guidance.
