Document Explorer is an interactive system that enables the exploration and labeling of large collections of text documents.

It uses a multi-scale visualization that keeps document attributes connected to document instances, making text review and labeling easier.

![Document Explorer teaser]({{site.url}}/images/vis_portfolio/document_explorer_teaser.png)

**Focus**: text analytics, labeling workflows, and multi-scale visualization.
